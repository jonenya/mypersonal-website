FAQs: Web Development in Kenya

How much does a website cost in Kenya?

A basic 5-page business website starts from KES 40,000. Custom e-commerce sites range from KES 120,000 to KES 500,000+, depending on features (M-Pesa, inventory, user accounts). We provide detailed quotes after a free consultation.

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard business website takes 4-6 weeks. Complex e-commerce or web applications take 2-4 months. Timelines depend on project scope, content readiness, and feedback speed.

Do you offer website hosting and maintenance?

Yes. We offer reliable, fast hosting on Kenyan servers (from KES 2,500/month) and monthly maintenance plans (from KES 5,000/month) covering updates, backups, security, and minor content changes.
